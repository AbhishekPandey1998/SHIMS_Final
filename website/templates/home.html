S.H.I.M.S.

State Health Information Management System

Our project entitled as “STATE HEALTH INFORMATION MANAGEMENT SYSTEM (SHIMS)” is a step to computerize the health data collection and management to support planning, management, and decision making in health facilities and organizations. And establish a complete electronic patient record immediately available to authorized clinicians and staff. This system also introduces paperless patient registration to increase accuracy, speed and patient service.



Electronic Health Data

The electronic medical record replaces the paper version of a patient’s medical history. The electronic health record includes more health data, test results, and treatments.

Easy Sharing with Healthcare Workers

It also is designed to share data with other electronic health records so other healthcare providers can access a patient’s healthcare data. Patient portals allow patients to access their personal health data such as medications and lab results over an internet connection.

Help making better policies

The health data, after due consent of the patient, could be shared with the policy makers, in order to analyse the data and make better health policies.
